Introduction

Hello, my name is Brandon Cruz and I am a System Administrator at Cornell University.

My story starts as a summer intern at BorgWarner Inc, working in the IT dept. I had no idea other than how to use a PC before I started and I was surprised to how much I had to learn! So for the next few summers, I shadowed everyone I could within the IT field from the Help Desk Admins, to the Programmers, to the Client Techs, and finally to the Server/Network Admins. I realized that in order to grow in this field, I had to pick a path and learn as much as possible and keep learning! I was hired in 2007 as a Client Tech from BorgWarner and spent the next 8 years learning new technologies and picking up certifications along the way.

As of recently in 2012, I have made a decision to further my career by enrolling at Western Governors University with an emphasis in Network Administration. It was through my previous work experience at BorgWarner that solidified my decision to pursue a new direction and career change. My interest in the Network Administration industry grew as I performed Networking, Server Administration, Testing and Troubleshooting skills on Server equipment and Manufacturing Systems.
